USS Defiant NX-74205

The USS Defiant (NX-74205) was a 24th century Federation
Defiant-class starship operated by Starfleet. This was the
prototype of the class and the second Federation ship
known to bear the name Defiant.

Weaponry

The armaments of the Defiant included
  • A total of four phaser cannon assemblies
  • The standard phaser beam emitters
  • a total of six torpedo launchers
  • deflector shields
  • ablative armor
  • cloacking device
