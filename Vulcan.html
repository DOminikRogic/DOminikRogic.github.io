Vulcan

Ni'Var, formerly Vulcan, was an inhabited M-class planet
in the Vulcan system of the Alpha Quadrant. It had no moons,
but appeared to have close planetary companions. It was the
homeworld of the Vulcans, a warp-capable humanoid species.

Location

The Vulcan system, or 40 Eridani, was an inhabited star
system located in the Alpha Quadrant. This was a trinary star
system. Several planets, including Delta Vega and Vulcan,
orbited the star 40 Eridani A. Neighboring the Andorian system,
it was located sixteen light years from the Sol system.
